1. Dynamic Warm-up & Activation

Focus on slow, controlled movements. We are waking up muscles, not fatiguing them.

    • Stand with your back against a wall. Arms in a "goalpost" position.
    • Slowly slide your arms up the wall, keeping your forearms and wrists in contact.
    • Focus on using your back muscles. No front delt pain.
    • Lie on your side, legs straight.
    • Slowly raise your top leg without rocking your torso.
    • Focus on feeling the muscle on the side of your hip contract. This isolates the glute medius.
    • Place a mini-band just above your knees.
    • Focus on actively pushing your knees out against the band throughout the entire squat.
    • This forces your glutes to activate.

2. Main Lift: Snatch (Technique Focus)

We are doing this first while you are fresh. The weight is light. The goal is perfect, crisp movement.

    • Focus on speed and a strong, stable catch position.
    • How does the right shoulder feel receiving the bar overhead?

3. Secondary Lift: Bench Press

We are using a controlled tempo to build stability in the shoulder.

    • Tempo: 3 seconds down, 1 second pause on chest, explosive up.
    • Keep your shoulder blades pulled back and down throughout the entire lift.
    • If you feel any sharp pain, stop the set.
